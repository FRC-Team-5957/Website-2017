    Role Definitions

    Lead: Leader of the sub team as labeled, or Leader of the program they know.
    Manager: Manage the team or site assigned to them.
    Assistant: Assissts the Leader of their sub team, or the Leader of the program they know.

    Inventor Builder: Builds the robot or robot parts in Inventor.
    Pit Engineer: Works on robot in between matches at the competition.
    Researcher: Researches items relative to the sub team they are in.
